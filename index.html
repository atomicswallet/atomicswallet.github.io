Atomic Wallet - Buy, Stake and Exchange in One Crypto Wallet

For feasible management of your crypto funds, you will eventually need storage that comes with high-end security features and a user-friendly interface. But, this is not all that we require from a wallet as there are some other functionalities that a user looks for. In modern-day crypto wallets, there are options for buying, exchanging, and staking cryptos and that's what Atomic wallet comes pre-equipped with. Yes, you read that right. Today, we are going to discuss all that is required for you to understand this wallet in a better way. And to begin with, we will define what this wallet is and then this should be followed by a detailed discussion on how to use it as a beginner.

Download Atomic wallet
As said already, the first thing that you need to do is download the Atomic wallet Chrome extension and to get the extension installed, follow the steps as explained here:

Note: The extension is available for operating systems such as Windows, MacOS, Ubuntu, Debian, and Fedora. Therefore, there is no such chrome extension but Windows device software.* Learn what a MATIC wallet is and how they work

* To start the process, go to the https://atomicwallet.io/ website
* Followed by this, click the "DOWNLOAD" button on the given page
* Now, click on the icon of your device's operating system
* Upon doing so, the wallet's .exe file will start to get installed on your device
* When the file gets downloaded, please open it and follow some easy installation prompts
* Now, open the installed file and then set up your wallet as instructed below

How to Set up your wallet

* As the first step, please click on the "NEW WALLET" button upon opening the wallet software
* Now, you have to choose an Atomic wallet login password and enter the same twice
* As you go on, click the "SET PASSWORD" button and then go to the next step
* Then a page with a 12-word seed phrase will open
* Here, on this page, you need to copy down this phrase or click "copy to clipboard"
* After backing it up, please click on the "OPEN WALLET" button
There you go. Your wallet is all set up and ready for eventual usage.

How do you stake crypto through an Atomic wallet?

For staking on Atomic wallet, here are the easy steps that you need to follow. By staking crypto assets, you can have a regular side income and earn crypto rewards while you are enjoying your time.

* To begin the procedure, sign in and access your wallet
* After this, you need to find and tap on the "Staking" icon present in the sidebar on the left
* Now, choose which crypto asset you wish to stake and then click on the "Stake" option
* Then, you can pick one of the available validators
* After making your final decision, please click on the "Stake" option again
* That's all you need to do to stake any of the desired crypto assets
* Is Atomic wallet safe?

The main reason why a lot of users opt for this wallet is because of the fact that it claims to maintain the complete anonymity of users' wallets as well as their profiles. Along with the feature of anonymity, it also makes sure that your private keys are encrypted. And most importantly, only legitimate users are allowed to use this wallet. Alongside that, the users' accounts are encrypted with AES and TLS encryption technology.

A glance at the Atomic wallet fees

When you first download the Atomic wallet on your device, the extension, or the mobile-friendly application, you are not charged any kind of usage fee. But, as you start using the trading features on it, you will be levied some charge for this. For example, if you carry out any operation through this Cryptocurrency wallet, you will be charged a 2% fee or $10 per transaction (whichever is higher). Before you make any kind of transaction through this wallet, make sure you have enough funds for paying the gas fee/operation fee. 

Deposit and withdraw from Polygon Wallet

The crypto industry has come a long way over the years. Nowadays, Polygon wallet apps are so user-friendly, anyone can figure it out. To deposit or withdraw Polygon using your wallet, just open up your app and find Polygon in the wallet section. You should then see buttons to deposit or withdraw Polygon. Simply choose an option and follow the instructions. If you want to deposit, a deposit address will be provided. Just copy and paste that address so the person sending your Polygon can do so. If you are withdrawing MATIC, then you need to copy and paste the address from the wallet on the receiving end of the transaction so you can send the Polygon there.
